Secondary Education Access and Quality in Malaysia

Insights into Student Demographics and Performance

Selangor has the highest number of students. This may due to its larger population size and greater development compared to other states.

The number of students in Selangor grew by 0.86% between 2016 and 2021.

Although Selangor has the highest number of students, its completion is not the highest.

Completion rate may exceed 100% due to students repeating grades or transferring between states.

Putrajaya has a low student population and completion rate as it is primarily an administrative center with fewer schools.

Insights into Teacher Demographics and Gender

As there are highest student population in Selangor from the analysis above, the teachers are also the highest overall. It is then followed by Johor.

The proportion of male teachers is greater than female teachers in each state.
